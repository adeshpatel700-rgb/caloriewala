# Privacy Policy for CalorieWala

**Last Updated:** February 5, 2026

## Introduction
CalorieWala ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Food Data**: Photos of meals, descriptions, and nutrition information you log
- **User Preferences**: App settings, calorie goals, and favorite meals
- **Device Information**: Camera permissions for food photography and barcode scanning

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Local Storage**: All your meal data is stored locally on your device using Hive database
- **Image Processing**: Food photos are processed locally using ML Kit (offline)
- **API Usage**: When using AI analysis features, food descriptions are sent to our AI service provider (Groq) for nutrition estimation

## How We Use Your Information
- Provide nutrition analysis for your meals
- Track your calorie and macro intake
- Display personalized statistics and trends
- Improve app functionality and user experience

## Data Storage and Security
- **Local Storage**: All personal data (meals, goals, history) is stored locally on your device
- **No Account Required**: We do not collect email, name, or any personal identifiers
- **Third-Party AI Service**: Food descriptions sent to Groq API are processed for nutrition analysis only and not stored permanently
- **Image Data**: Photos you take are stored locally; we never upload them to our servers

## Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services:
- **Groq API**: For AI-powered nutrition analysis (text-based only, no images)
- **Google ML Kit**: For local on-device image recognition (completely offline)

## Your Rights
You have the right to:
- Delete all your data by clearing app data in device settings
- Stop using the app at any time
- Deny camera permissions (app will still work with manual input)

## Data Sharing
We do NOT:
- Share your personal data with third parties
- Sell your information
- Upload your food photos anywhere
- Require account creation or email addresses

## Children's Privacy
Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
